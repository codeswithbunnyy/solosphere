Hi, I'm Preet Patel

Hello there, My name is Preet, and I'm an Indian designer that has a strong desire to push the frontiers of invention and creativity. My passion for design stems from an unyielding goal to produce innovative and powerful ideas that not only grab attention but also leave a lasting impression.
Using my meticulous attention to detail and innovative spirit, I work hard to transform concepts into powerful visual narratives. Working with amazing businesses like Pinnacle, Vertex, and Serenity has been a delight, and I've enjoyed bringing my enthusiasm to their distinctive visual stories.

MY PROCESS
01
Immersion
I begin by thoroughly exploring the world of your brand. To create anything truly meaningful, you must first understand your company's beliefs, objectives, and identity.
02
Strategy
I create a unique branding approach. To make sure your brand not only stands out but resonates, this entails market study, target audience identification, and strategic differentiation.
03
Design
This is the point at which magic occurs. I give concepts life by turning them into visually striking designs. Every aspect is taken into account, from creating original logos to selecting color schemes and fonts with care.
04
Implementation
I make sure that every touchpoint for your brand is consistent. We handle the uniform application of your new brand image online and off, giving your audience a cohesive experience.
05
Launch
Lastly, I'm happy to announce the debut of your new logo. We strive to make sure that the world views your brand in a positive light, whether it be through a product launch, an effective digital campaign, or a revamped online presence.
